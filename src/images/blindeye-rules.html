..Blind Eye..

2 Players

A game designed to be played with a high amount of random chance and low strategy input.


..Objective..

The end goal is to obtain the highest number of points after 9 rounds of gameplay. The game has been constructed so that a draw is not possible for the final score.


Each win is worth 1 Point


..Equipment..

1x Standard Deck of Playing Cards (Joker’s Removed)

..Alternate Gameplay..

You can scale the difficulty of gameplay by adding or subtracting the number of cards delt to the player and the middle of the table.


Minimal Difficulty – 3 CARDS (FU1, FD2, FD3)

Normal Difficulty – 5 CARDS (FU1, FD2, FU3, FD4, FD5)

Increased Difficulty – 7 CARDS (FU1, FD2, FU3, FD4, FU5, FD6, FD7)


*FU = Face Up

*FD = Face Down
